## Key Points
- Does the bible mention free will or choice?
- The Bible doesn’t use the exact modern phrase “free will” in the philosophical sense, but the idea of **choice** and human responsibility is woven throughout Scripture.
- Here are some key places: --- ### 1.
- Explicit mentions of **choice** - **Deuteronomy 30:19** – *“I have set before you life and death, blessing and curse.
- Therefore **choose life**, that you and your offspring may live.”* → Israel is called to make a real choice in response to God’s covenant.
- - **Joshua 24:15** – *“Choose this day whom you will serve … But as for me and my house, we will serve the Lord.”* → Joshua presents service to Adonai as a matter of decision.
