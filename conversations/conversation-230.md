# Jesus as Son of Righteousness

## Summary
is Jesus known as the son of righteousness Yes, Jesus is referred to as the "Sun of Righteousness" in Malachi 4:2 in the Bible, which is often interpreted by Christians as a prophetic reference to Him. The term symbolizes light, healing, and righteousness, reflecting Jesus' role as the bringer of salvation and spiritual illumination.
