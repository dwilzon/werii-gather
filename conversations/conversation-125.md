## Key Points
- what temp should i cool new york strip If you’re cooling (resting or storing) a cooked New York strip steak: - **After cooking:** - Let it rest **5–10 minutes** on the counter loosely covered with foil.
- This allows juices to redistribute.
- - If you’re not eating it soon, let it cool to about **room temperature (around 75 °F / 24 °C)** before refrigerating.
- - **For refrigeration:** - Refrigerate once it’s no longer hot — ideally within **2 hours of cooking**.
- - Store at **≤ 40 °F (4 °C)** in an airtight container or wrapped tightly.
- - **For food safety:** - Don’t leave it in the “danger zone” (40–140 °F / 4–60 °C) for more than 2 hours total.
