## Key Points
- What are sailpoint functional roles In SailPoint IdentityNow (and IdentityIQ), **functional roles** are predefined or dynamic groupings of entitlements (access rights) that represent a **business function or job responsibility**.
- They allow identity governance teams to model, assign, and manage access in a way that aligns with how the business actually operates.

- - **Assigned to identities** to give them the access they need to perform a specific function (e.g., HR Generalist, IT Support, Finance Analyst).
- - Often **used for access reviews**, policy enforcement, and birthright access provisioning.
