## Key Points
- can ace deck and bleach sprayer and ospho It sounds like you’re asking whether you can use an **ACE deck sprayer / bleach sprayer** to apply **Ospho** (a phosphoric acid–based rust converter).
- Here’s what you should know: --- ### Compatibility - **Sprayer type:** Most inexpensive pump sprayers marketed for bleach or deck cleaner are made with plastics, seals, and nozzles that can handle mild acids and harsh cleaners.
- - **Ospho:** It’s phosphoric acid–based, so it’s more acidic than bleach but not nearly as caustic as strong hydrochloric or sulfuric acids.
- Many people do apply it with garden or deck sprayers successfully.
- --- ### Best Practices - **Check materials:** Look for sprayers labeled “acid resistant” or rated for use with cleaners and chemicals.
- Bleach sprayers often have Viton seals, which hold up well against phosphoric acid.
