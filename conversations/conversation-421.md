- hiriq in hebrew **Hiriq** (חִירִיק) is one of the **Hebrew niqqud** (vowel points), and it represents the vowel sound **/i/** — like the “ee” in **"machine"** or the "i" in **"pizza"**.
- ### Hiriq – Basic Info: - **Symbol**: A single dot **below** the letter → **ִ** - **Pronunciation**: Like the English **"ee"** sound - **Example**: - **בִּית** (*beit*) – "house" The dot under **בּ** is the **hiriq**, making the **"bi"** sound.
