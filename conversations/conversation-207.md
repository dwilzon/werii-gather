## Key Points
- why can’t i find a recent facebook video i was just watching There are a few common reasons why a Facebook video you just watched can suddenly be hard to find: ### 1.
- **Algorithm Refresh** When you refresh your feed or leave the video, Facebook’s algorithm may not show it again, even if it was recent.
- The feed is constantly re-ordered.
- **It Was a Story or Reel** If the video was a *Story* or *Reel*, it may have expired (Stories last 24 hours) or become buried in your Reels feed.
- **Privacy or Deletion** The person or page who posted it might have deleted it, or changed privacy settings so it’s no longer visible.
- **Not Saved or Followed** If you didn’t *like*, *share*, *comment*, or *save* the video, Facebook doesn’t always keep it easy to find in your history.
