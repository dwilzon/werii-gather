imanuel meaning in hebrew The Hebrew name **עִמָּנוּאֵל** (*ʿImmanuʾel*) is made of three parts: - **עִם** (*ʿim*) = “with” - **נוּ** (*nu*) = “us” - **אֵל** (*El*) = “God” So the meaning is **“God [is] with us.”** This name appears in **Isaiah 7:14** — “Behold, the young woman shall conceive and bear a son, and shall call his name Immanuel” — and is picked up again in **Matthew 1:23** when applied to Yeshua (Jesus).
