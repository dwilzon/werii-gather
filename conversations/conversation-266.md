# Intercessor word in Greek

## Summary
what's the word for intercessor in the New Testament related to Jesus in Greek The Greek word used in the New Testament for "intercessor" in relation to Jesus is **"paraklētos"** (παράκλητος). This term is often translated as "advocate," "helper," or "comforter," particularly in contexts referring to Jesus and the Holy Spirit.
