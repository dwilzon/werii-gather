- - **לֶאֱהֹב (le’ehov)** – to love.
- - **לִשְׂנֹא (lisno)** – to hate.
- - **מִלְחָמָה (milḥamah)** – war, battle, struggle.
- - **שָׁלוֹם (shalom)** – peace, wholeness, harmony.
