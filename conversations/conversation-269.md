## Key Points
- What is biblical wisdom and understanding In the Bible, **wisdom** and **understanding** are closely related but not identical.
- Together, they describe how a person rightly perceives, lives out, and applies the ways of Elohim.
- --- ### **Wisdom (חָכְמָה – Chokhmah)** - **Meaning:** Practical skill in living according to God’s ways.
- It is more than knowledge—it is applied truth.
- - **Biblical Picture:** Wisdom is often described as something to be sought after like treasure (Proverbs 2:4).
- It is not just intellectual, but moral and spiritual, guiding choices, relationships, and actions.
