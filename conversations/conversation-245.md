## Key Points
- how do you take care of your calluses on your hand To take care of calluses on your hands: 1.
- **Soak Your Hands:** Soak them in warm, soapy water for 10-15 minutes to soften the skin.
- **Gently Exfoliate:** Use a pumice stone or a gentle foot file to rub the callus in circular motions.
- **Moisturize:** Apply a thick moisturizer or hand cream, preferably with ingredients like urea or salicylic acid.
- **Protect:** Wear gloves when doing activities that cause friction, like lifting weights or gardening.
- **Manage Friction:** Adjust your technique or use protective gear to prevent further buildup.
