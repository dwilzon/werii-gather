- - **Nut**: This is the small hard piece located at the top of the guitar neck, right where the fretboard meets the headstock.
- It supports and spaces the strings at the end closest to the tuning pegs, sets their height above the fretboard, and marks one end of the vibrating length of each open string citeturn0search20.
- - **Saddle**: Found on the bridge of the guitar, this piece supports the strings at the opposite end, transmitting vibrations to the soundboard and helping determine string height (action) and intonation citeturn0search2turn0search6.
