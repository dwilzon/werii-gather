## Key Points
- how to respond to a widow who just lost her husband and their anniversary and his birthday just passed That’s a tender moment, and your response can make a big difference.
- Here are a few approaches you might take, depending on how close you are to the widow and what feels most natural for you: --- ### 1.
- Acknowledge the Pain and Milestones - *“I know this time must feel especially heavy with both your anniversary and his birthday having just passed.
- I’m so sorry you’re going through this.”* This shows you see her grief in the context of the recent dates, which is often validating.
- Offer Presence, Not Solutions - *“I can’t imagine how hard this is for you, but please know you’re not alone.
- I’m here whenever you need someone to talk to or just sit with.”* Grief doesn’t need fixing—what she’ll value most is knowing she’s supported.
