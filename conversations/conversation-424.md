- **Organize Your Learning Journal** Use **OneNote** or a physical/digital dot journal with sections: - **Vocabulary Bank**: New words with *nikkud*, pronunciation, simple + contextual sentence, root (שורש), and image if helpful.
- - **Grammar & Patterns**: Short entries on verb structures (binyanim), prefixes, suffixes, gender rules, etc.
- - **Daily Study Logs**: What you learned, what stuck, what didn’t, and a few practice sentences.
- - **Verse Study**: Hebrew Bible verses broken down with grammar notes and commentary.
