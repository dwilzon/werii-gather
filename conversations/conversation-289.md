## Key Points
- what is pain behind my eyes that goes away when I touch and push on my eyes and also goes away with good sleep Pain behind your eyes that **improves with pressure** and **goes away with good sleep** can have a few possible explanations.
- Some common ones are: ### 1.
- Eye Strain (Asthenopia) - Often happens from too much screen time, reading, or focusing without breaks.
- - Muscles that control eye focus get fatigued, leading to pressure or pain behind the eyes.
- - Rest or sleep relaxes the muscles, relieving the discomfort.
- - Pressing on your eyes can temporarily change pressure in the orbit and relax those muscles.
