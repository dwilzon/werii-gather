## Key Points
- Why would someone say “Are you not going to fix that thing” instead of “are you going to fix that thing” Good question!
- The difference comes down to **tone, perspective, and expectation**: ### 1.
- Challenging Tone** - **“Are you going to fix that thing?”** → Neutral.
- Just asking if the person plans to fix it.
- - **“Are you *not* going to fix that thing?”** → Implies surprise, impatience, or disapproval that the person might *not* fix it.
- It’s less neutral and more challenging.
