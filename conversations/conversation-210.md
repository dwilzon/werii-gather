## Key Points
- what comes after okta professional and administrator certs After the **Okta Certified Professional** and **Okta Certified Administrator** certifications, the next step is to pursue the **Okta Certified Consultant** or **Okta Certified Developer** certification
- **Okta Certified Consultant** - **Audience:** IAM engineers, solution architects, and implementation specialists.
- - **Focus:** Deep knowledge of complex Okta deployments, multi-org integrations, advanced directory and identity store setups, federation (OIDC, SAML), lifecycle management, and advanced troubleshooting.
- - **Why it fits after Admin:** It validates your ability to *design and implement* Okta in enterprise-scale environments, beyond day-to-day administration.
- **Okta Certified Developer** - **Audience:** Developers building custom applications and integrations with Okta.
- - **Focus:** Okta APIs, SDKs, OAuth, OIDC flows, custom authorization, SCIM provisioning, and embedding Okta authentication into apps.
