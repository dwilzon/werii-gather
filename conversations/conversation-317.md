## Key Points
- Do you need to wash new underwear that you buy from the store that’s in a box Yes — even if the underwear is sealed in a box, it’s best to wash it before wearing.
- Here’s why: - **Manufacturing residues** – Fabrics can have leftover dyes, finishing chemicals, and starches that can irritate skin.
- - **Handling during packaging** – Items are often handled by multiple people and machinery in factories and warehouses before boxing.
- - **Potential allergens or dust** – Cardboard boxes and storage areas can collect dust, lint, and allergens.
- - **Hygiene peace of mind** – Washing removes anything unseen that may have come from shipping or storage.
- A quick wash on a gentle cycle with mild detergent is enough — it’s more about removing chemicals and dust than heavy cleaning.
